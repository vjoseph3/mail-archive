Usage:

     mail-archive [-options] [files...]

In case of ambiguity, terminate the options with --


Options:

-s --set
Prompt to set the email account or alias under which to archive emails

-c --show-current
Print the email account or alias under which emails are currently being archived

-a --show-all
Print a list of all email accounts or aliases under which archived emails exist

--help
Print this message
